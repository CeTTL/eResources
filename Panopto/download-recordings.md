# Download recordings

## How to download a Panopto recording

**Recommended browsers:** Edge, Firefox, Safari, Chrome.

## On a computer

1. Go to [waikato.au.panopto.com](https://waikato.au.panopto.com)
2. On the top right corner, click on **sign in**.
